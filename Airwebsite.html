Air Quality Matters

BreatheEasy provides you with accurate and reliable air quality data. You can easily upload your air quality measurements and view real-time air quality index (AQI) readings for Vietnam. Our website is inspired by the USGS website and aims to be a valuable resource for air quality measurements, prioritizing ease of navigation, accessibility, community engagement, and environmental monitoring.

Everyone is invited to contribute to our community-driven effort by using their own sensors to upload air quality measurements. This initiative aims to make air quality data more transparent and accessible, particularly in Vietnam, helping everyone make informed decisions about their health and environment.
